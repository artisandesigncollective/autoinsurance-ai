# AutoInsurance AI — Pain Points

## Primary Pain Point
> **"My car was totaled. The insurance company offered me $12,000 but I can't find an identical replacement for less than $16,000. I'm being robbed while I'm vulnerable."**

### 1. CCC ONE Lowballing
- **Problem:** Insurers use "CCC ONE" software that selects lower-priced comps from far away and subtracts "condition adjustments" unfairly.
- **Scale:** Most total-loss offers are 15-30% below actual replacement cost.

### 2. The "Appraisal Clause" Secret
- **Problem:** Most policies have a clause that allows you to hire your own appraiser if you disagree on value, but insurers never tell you.
- **Our Solution:** AI invokes this clause automatically in the demand letter.

### 3. Replacement Friction
- **Problem:** Users need a car NOW to get to work. They accept the low offer just to get the check.
- **Our Solution:** We provide the proof in 60 seconds so they can negotiate an extra $3k+ immediately.

## Current Alternatives
1. **Accept the offer** (80%) — Total loss for the consumer.
2. **Public Appraisers**: Charge $300-$500 upfront.
3. **SueBot**: Our other project, but AutoInsurance is the specialized tool for this niche.
4. **Lawyers**: Don't take total loss cases because the "damages" ($3k-$5k) are too small for them. Perfect for AI.
