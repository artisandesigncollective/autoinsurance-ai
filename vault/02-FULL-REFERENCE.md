# AutoInsurance AI — Business Plan & PRD

## Market
- **TAM:** 6M+ cars totaled in the US annually.
- **SAM:** 1M+ owners of late-model cars who are most likely to fight a lowball.
- **SOM Year 1:** 1,800 users = $268,200 ARR.

## Revenue: $149 Flat Fee
- Unit Economics: Revenue $149, Stripe $4.62, LLM $1.00, **Margin: $143.38 (96%)**.
